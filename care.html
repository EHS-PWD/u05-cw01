Enclosure

Provide a spacious enclosure with a substrate suitable for digging, such as topsoil or coconut coir. Ensure the enclosure is secure to prevent escapes.

Diet

Russian tortoises are herbivores. Feed them a variety of leafy greens like kale, collard greens, and dandelion greens. Avoid fruits and high-protein foods.

Health

Keep the environment clean and provide access to UVB light to maintain their health. Regularly check for signs of illness, such as lethargy or swollen eyes.
